Rei K. Books

About

Rei K. is an Indonesian author who loves writing exciting action-adventure stories, heartwarming tales of friendship and family, and emotional journeys that touch your soul. With a dash of angst and drama, Rei’s stories often explore themes like brotherhood, sibling rivalry, absent parents, and the dynamics of dysfunctional families.

Genre

Light Novel

A fast-paced, illustrated novel style with manga-inspired art, perfect for readers who love a blend of action, humor, and relatable young characters.

Shonen

Exciting, action-packed stories often featuring young protagonists on journeys of growth, courage, and adventure, aiming to inspire and entertain.

Young Adult (YA)

Stories focused on the experiences, dreams, and challenges of teenage characters, reflecting the real emotions of growing up, friendship, and self-discovery.

Urban Fantasy

Stories set in a modern-day world where mythical creatures and supernatural elements blend seamlessly with everyday life, creating a unique, otherworldly twist on familiar settings.

Action/Adventure

High-energy plots filled with daring quests, thrilling battles, and unexpected challenges that keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Drama/Family

Emotional, character-driven tales that explore the depth of relationships, resilience, and family bonds, adding heart and meaning to every adventure.

Book Series

The Masamune Clan Series

In a ninja world full of intrigue, Tadashi Masamune fights to defend the honor of his clan. Hardened since childhood, he uses all his strength to overcome the betrayal that threatens both his clan and the kingdom.

However, when the dark secrets of his family’s past are revealed, Tadashi must navigate complicated clan politics and make difficult choices. Will Tadashi succeed in protecting his clan and discovering the true meaning of family, or will ambition and revenge destroy everything?

Themes: Action, Drama, Family

KazuAiko Angsty Sweet Romance Series

A collection of emotionally charged romance stories where love is messy, raw, and beautifully real. Kazuki and Aiko are the main characters, but each book tells a different story and can be read in any order.

Co-written with Aisuru H.

Genre: Angsty Sweet Romance
Themes: Childhood Sweethearts, Friends to Lovers, Pretend Relationships, and more.
Age Rating: 13+

Available Languages:

  • English

Lichte Cozy Mysteries Series

Meet Dai Nakamura, a high school DJ who knows more about geography than he does about sleep. Between juggling side hustles to make ends meet and harboring a secret crush on his best friend, life is never dull. When a mishap leads to a debt owed to an arrogant freelance photographer, Dai finds himself thrown into a whirlwind of hilarious misadventures and thrilling mysteries.

Can he unravel the secrets behind each case, face unexpected enemies, and maybe, just maybe, confess his feelings before it’s too late?

Genre: Cozy Mystery
Themes: Action, Humor, Family, Light Romance
Age Rating: 13+

Available Languages:

  • English
