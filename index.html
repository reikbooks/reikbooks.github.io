About

Rei K. is an Indonesian author of light novels, shonen, YA, and urban fantasy. She writes stories about brotherhood, family secrets, and the bonds that hold us together even when everything falls apart.

The Masamune Clan series — a 14-book saga set in feudal Japan — is her flagship work, exploring ninja clans, hidden identities, and the weight of legacy.

Beyond writing, Rei K. is passionate about making stories accessible to readers in multiple languages, with Masamune Clan available in 8 languages.
