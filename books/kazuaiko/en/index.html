KazuAiko Angsty Sweet Romance Series [EN]

About the Series:

KazuAiko Angsty Sweet Romance Series is a collection of emotionally charged romance stories where love is messy, raw, and beautifully real. Kazuki and Aiko are the main characters, but each book tells a different story and can be read in any order.

About the Authors:

Rei K. crafts emotionally charged stories of longing, heartbreak, and the quiet ache of unspoken words. Aisuru H. writes heartwarming romance about love, healing, and happy endings. Together, they bring the KazuAiko Angsty Sweet Romance Series to life.
