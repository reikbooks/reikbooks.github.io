Lichte Cozy Mysteries

Meet Dai Nakamura — part-time DJ, full-time mystery magnet.

Meet Dai Nakamura, a high school DJ who knows more about geography than he does about sleep. Between juggling side hustles to make ends meet and harboring a secret crush on his best friend, life is never dull.

When a mishap leads to a debt owed to an arrogant freelance photographer, Dai finds himself thrown into a whirlwind of hilarious misadventures and thrilling mysteries.

Can he unravel the secrets behind each case, face unexpected enemies, and maybe, just maybe, confess his feelings before it's too late?

Genre: Cozy Mystery

Themes: Action, Humor, Family, Light Romance

Age Rating: 13+
