The Masamune Clan

In a world of ninja intrigue, Tadashi Masamune struggles to uphold his clan's honor.

Forged in hardship since childhood, he exerts all his strength to overcome betrayals that threaten both clan and kingdom. However, when dark secrets of his family's past are unveiled, Tadashi must navigate complex clan politics and make difficult choices.

Will Tadashi succeed in protecting his clan and discovering the true meaning of family, or will ambition and vengeance destroy everything?

Themes: Action, Drama, Family

Age Rating: 13+
