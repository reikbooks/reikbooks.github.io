The Masamune Clan

In a ninja world full of intrigue, Tadashi Masamune fights to defend the honor of his clan.

About the Series

A 14-book saga set in feudal Japan. The Masamune Clan follows Tadashi, Shiro, Raito, and a cast of warriors navigating loyalty, betrayal, family secrets, and the weight of duty. Action, drama, brotherhood — and a mystery that spans generations.

Genre: Light Novel, Shonen, Action/Drama, Family

Reading order: Start from Book 1 — The Heir's Burden
